Winter Wiper Tips

  • Lift wipers off the windshield before snowstorms to prevent freezing to the glass
  • Never use wipers to clear ice — scrape first, then wipe
  • Consider switching to winter-specific blades November–March
  • Apply Rain-X or similar hydrophobic treatment before winter
