🌧️ Our Take

Bosch Icon is the best wiper blade you can buy. It's been our top pick for years and nothing has dethroned it. The combination of wipe quality, durability, and noise level is unmatched at any price.

Rain-X Latitude is the best value. The water-repellent coating is a genuine advantage, not a gimmick. If you want 90% of premium performance at a budget price, this is it.

PIAA Super Silicone is the buy-it-and-forget-it pick. If you hate replacing wipers, the silicone rubber lasts nearly twice as long as conventional rubber. Worth the premium if convenience matters more than upfront cost.
